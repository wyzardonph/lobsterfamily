But how do we get these live lobsters from the ocean to your plate, often across continents? The answer lies in a meticulous process that prioritizes both speed and the well-being of the lobsters. From Trap to Tank: A Journey in Chilling Care The lobstermen use traditional traps, ensuring minimal impact on the delicate ocean floor ecosystem. Once caught, the lobsters are carefully transferred to specially designed holding tanks on the boats. These tanks are filled with constantly circulating seawater kept at a cool temperature, mimicking the lobsters' natural habitat. Packing for the Big Voyage: A Lobster's First-Class Flight Upon reaching the harbor, the lobsters are meticulously sorted by size and health. Only the most robust and lively specimens make it to our packing facility. Here, insulated and temperature-controlled boxes are prepared. These boxes are lined with a special seaweed-based material that retains moisture and provides a natural environment for the lobsters. We also include gel packs specifically designed to keep the temperature cool and stable during the journey. The Art of Lobster Logistics: Speed Meets Sustainability The Lobster Shack understands that time is of the essence. We work closely with international airfreight companies to ensure the fastest possible delivery times. This minimizes stress on the lobsters and guarantees you receive your crustacean companions in peak condition. However, speed doesn't compromise our commitment to sustainability. We partner with airlines that prioritize fuel-efficient practices and carbon offsetting programs. Live or Cooked? The Choice is Yours We offer the ultimate in customization. You can choose to receive your lobsters live, ready for a home-cooked culinary adventure. We provide detailed information and even instructional videos on humanely preparing live lobsters. For those who prefer convenience, we offer pre-cooked lobster tails, flash-frozen at the peak of freshness to lock in all the flavor and texture. These tails are perfect for a quick and impressive meal, ready to be grilled, roasted, or incorporated into delectable dishes.
